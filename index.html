Who are we?

The Computer Science Research Group is committed to provide the solutions of various real world problems through digital technologies. The group works on a number of domains of computer science and other collaborative tasks. A number of tools are developed which can be accessed for research purpose. All the tools and techniques developed by the group are freely available for non-commercial purposes. The group also welcomes the research discussions.
